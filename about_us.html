Our History

Since 2003, GemSelect.com has been selling gemstones to customers all over the world. We've had the pleasure of providing quality gemstones to jewelers and gemstone collectors in over 120 countries over the past decade and we would like to thank you and all of our other loyal, satisfied customers for giving us the opportunity to be a part of your jewelry making experiences. We are extremely grateful for the long-standing relationships we've built over the past 15+ years, with customers who include many of the world's finest jewelry stores, designers, artists, suppliers and resellers.
